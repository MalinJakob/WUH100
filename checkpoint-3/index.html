London

Why I love London

London is my favorite city because of its incredible musicals. From Wicked to Billy Elliot, there’s always a great show. You can enjoy a new musical every night, making it the perfect place for musical lovers!

To Do in London

In London, you can explore iconic landmarks like the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, and the British Museum. Enjoy world-class theatre in the West End, stroll through beautiful parks, shop at Covent Garden or Oxford Street, and take a ride on the London Eye for stunning views.

Gothenburg

Why I live in Gothenburg

I live in Gothenburg because my great love is here, and the city’s vibe really resonates with me. I enjoy the mix of urban life and nature, and it’s also where I’m pursuing my education. Gothenburg feels like the perfect place to build my future.

To Do in Gothenburg

In Gothenburg, you can explore beautiful parks like Slottsskogen, visit the Gothenburg Museum of Art, or stroll along the picturesque canals. Enjoy shopping at Nordstan, try delicious seafood at Feskekörka, and have fun at Liseberg amusement park. The city's lively cafés and festivals offer something for everyone.
